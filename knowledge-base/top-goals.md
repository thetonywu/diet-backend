# Top Goals People Have on the Animal-Based Diet

Research compiled from Paul Saladino MD's YouTube channel, podcast, and online content.

## Body Composition & Performance

- [ ] Lose weight and get lean naturally
- [ ] Build muscle and improve fitness
- [ ] Stay properly hydrated
- [ ] Improve flexibility and mobility
- [ ] Optimize exercise and recovery protocols

## Hormonal & Metabolic Health

- [ ] Boost testosterone naturally
- [ ] Optimize hormones broadly (thyroid, cortisol, estrogen, etc.)
- [ ] Reverse insulin resistance
- [ ] Improve thyroid health
- [ ] Fix metabolic dysfunction

## Longevity & Disease Prevention

- [ ] Maximize lifespan and healthspan (live to 120+)
- [ ] Reverse or prevent chronic disease
- [ ] Improve heart health and prevent cardiovascular disease
- [ ] Reduce cancer risk
- [ ] Optimize blood work markers

## Gut, Skin & Hair

- [ ] Fix gut health issues (bloating, IBS, leaky gut)
- [ ] Improve hair growth and stop hair loss
- [ ] Clear up skin issues (acne, eczema, psoriasis)
- [ ] Reduce inflammation

## Fertility & Women's Health

- [ ] Improve male fertility
- [ ] Improve female fertility
- [ ] Support a healthy pregnancy
- [ ] Optimize postpartum recovery
- [ ] Balance women's hormones

## Sleep & Mental Health

- [ ] Sleep better and fall asleep faster
- [ ] Improve energy levels throughout the day
- [ ] Improve mental clarity and focus
- [ ] Reduce brain fog
- [ ] Improve mood and reduce anxiety/depression

## Detox & Clean Living

- [ ] Remove toxins from home and kitchen
- [ ] Switch to non-toxic personal care and hygiene products
- [ ] Reduce EMF exposure
- [ ] Find safe water sources
- [ ] Live a more ancestral lifestyle
- [ ] Reduce exposure to harmful chemicals in food supply
